I am an incoming Master's student at ETH Zurich in the MSc Data Science program. Nowadays, I work in AI research by tackling fundamental problems with strong relevance to industrial applications.

Previously, I completed my Bachelor of Science with a double major in Mathematics and Economics at Ecole Polytechnique in Paris. My previous experiences include:

  • Research Internship at Huawei Technologies Paris Research Center where I worked on neural network training algorithms that allow model flexibility in both centralized and federated training setups.
  • Research Internship at SciencesPo where I developed an OpenAI gym environment named UberGym for multi-agent reinforcement learning in ride-hailing with autonomous cars under the EQUIPRICE grant of Prof. Dr. Alfred Galichon.
  • Data Scientist at Olympos where I worked with state-of-the-art LLMs with LinkedIn data in order to help startups with their recruiting process using human-in-the-loop AI.
  • Member of the National Physics Olympiads team of Turkey in 2019, 2020, and 2021 where I travelled to Vietnam, Australia, Israel, Russia for free and won a few medals.

I will spend the summer of 2023 as Quantitative Trading Intern at Susquehanna International Group in Dublin. Feel free to reach out if you're doing something exciting with AI or just to have a random chat!
