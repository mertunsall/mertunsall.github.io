Mert Unsal

I am a Master's student at ETH Zurich in the MSc Data Science program. I work as a research assistant at SRI Lab. I am interested in researching AI4Math, specifically doing program search and autoformalization using transformers and LLMs by leveraging synthetic data.

Previously, I did my Bachelor of Science with a double major in Mathematics and Economics at Ecole Polytechnique in Paris. My previous experiences include:

  • Research Internship at Huawei Technologies Paris Research Center where I worked on flexible neural network training algorithms and presented the work in NeurIPS 2023.
  • Quantitative Trading Intern at SIG where I worked on option pricing with stochastic volatility models.
  • Data Scientist at Olympos where I worked with state-of-the-art LLMs with LinkedIn data in order to help startups with their recruiting process using human-in-the-loop AI.
  • Member of the National Physics Olympiads team of Turkey in 2019, 2020, and 2021 where I travelled to Vietnam, Australia, Israel, Russia and won a few medals.
